Long Island Iced Tea

Curiously, there is no tea in this drink.

Ingredients

  • 30 ml lemon juice
  • 15 ml gin
  • 15 ml rum
  • 15 ml tequila
  • 15 ml triple sec
  • 15 ml vodka
  • 30 ml simple syrup
  • Can of Coca-Cola
  • Ice

Instructions
