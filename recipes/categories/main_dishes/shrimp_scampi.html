Shrimp Scampi

Can't go wrong with pasta!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb shrimp
  • 2.5 tbspn butter
  • 1 tbspn olive oil
  • 3 gloves of minced garlic
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1 lemon
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • Salt and black pepper

Instructions

Firstly, heat your butter and olive oil over medium heat in a pan until the butter is fully melted and bubbling. Add in garlic and pepper flakes if desired. Stir until it is well-mixed and let it sit for about 30 seconds, with care take to not burn the garlic.
Increase heat to medium high and add the white wine and lemon juice. When it starts boiling, lay out the shrimp in the pan evenly and allow it to cook for 2-3 minutes then flip the shrimp and cook for 1-2 minutes. Remove from the heat when shrimp is done and season with salt and pepper to taste. Add in parsley and mix well.


Add your desired pasta noodles (any kind works here, it's really up to personal preference) and enjoy!
