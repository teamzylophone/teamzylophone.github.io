Tomato and Eggs Stir Fry

Flavor text here. Pun intended.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 6 oz of ketchup (Fast food ketchup packets are perfect here!)
  • 2-4 tomatoes
  • 2 scallions
  • Quarter clove of garlic
  • Salt
  • 1 cup of water

Instructions

Firstly, you're gonna start off by cracking your eggs into a separate bowl and stir them thoroughly. Roughly mince your garlic. Chop your scallions into pieces approximately 1mm thick. Be sure to separate the green and white parts! The white parts will cook slower than the green parts and should be put in the pan separately. Dice your tomatoes to approximately 1 inch pieces and set them aside. Get your pan and set it on the stovetop at medium-high heat with a light covering of oil.


Once your pan is hot, put your garlic and white scallions into the pan and stir them gently. You can choose to put your tomatoes in now if you prefer a more roasted flavor, otherwise throw them in when you add the eggs. Adjust the heat if needed to avoid burning the garlic. Once the aromas start flowing, pour the water and ketchup into the pan. Be careful to not burn yourself! About 15-20 seconds later, pour in your eggs while mixing it at the same time to avoid large clumps from forming. Throw in your tomatoes if you haven't already. Add in your green scallions and season with salt to taste. As the eggs finish cooking to your desired consistency, turn off the heat and plate your tomato and egg stir fry.
