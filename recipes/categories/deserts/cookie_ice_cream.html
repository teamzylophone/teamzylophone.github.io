Chocolate Chip Cookie Ice Cream Sandwiches

Not a good idea for diets!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 4 oz dark chocolate coarsely chopped
  • Sea salt (optional)
  • Ice cream of your choice of flavor (recipe for ice cream coming soon!)

Instructions

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit and line two baking sheets with parchment paper or do these in batches on a single baking sheet. In a medium sized bowl add the butter. In a separate bowl whisk together the egg and egg yolk. Stir the sugar into the butter until thoroughly incorporated and then add the baking soda, salt and vanilla extract and stir until completely mixed. Add the whisked egg and egg yolk to the mixture and stir more until completely incorporated. Fold in the chopped chocolate until well incorporated.
With a cookie scoop or spoon drop 1 1/2 - 2 tablespoon sized mounds of cookie dough on to the parchment lined baking sheet spacing them about 2 1/2 inches apart. This should allow you to only have 6 per cookie sheet. Place one baking sheet into the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes then remove them and let cool slightly for 5-7 minutes. With a spatula, transfer them to a cooling rack to let them finish cooling completely. As the cookies are cooling, but still warm, sprinkle them with a little bit of flakey sea salt. Repeat this step with another baking sheet or with the same baking sheet until all the the cookie dough is used.


Use a ice cream scoop to scoop out half a sphere of ice cream and place onto a cookie. Cover with a second cookie and wrap it with food wrap. Place in freezer to firm up the ice cream before serving.
